About

Halloj!
I'm a passionate photographer living and working from my home studio in Mölndal. I dabble with many styles of photography, ranging from capturing night scenes on analogue film to digital wedding photography. While I do pride myself with my knowledge in photography and lighting that is not what drives me. The one thing I truly love is photographing people! As a portrait photographer you fortunate enough to get to meet and know new interesting people. A photo session is truly a conversation, a conversation between the sitter and the photographer, a conversation brief in time yet captured forever. It is truly a rewarding experience to laugh and work together and I love every moment of it.

Ha det gött!
Lukas Rahmn

Portrait sessions & Wedding photography

I regularly accept bookings for photo sessions, and if you think think I could help you I would love to hear from you! I do both on location shoots and studio photography of which you can see a collection on my portraiture page. I focus on creating interesting and creative images together with my subjects. My belief is that creating a creative yet comfortable atmosphere is the key to capturing interesting and evocative imagery and it underpins my entire workflow. A truly successful photo session is one where you don't just walk away with amazing images, but enjoyed every moment spent creating them.

Since my work varies quite a lot and no two assignment are the same I don't have any fixed pricing, I would ask that you contact me so I can understand your needs and prepare a quote. Good to know is that I'm of course I'm registered fo F-Skatt.

If you have any questions feel free to contact me! If you prefer to talk I also provide free pre-booking video/phone consultation sessions.

I'd love to hear from you!

Print sales

If you are interested in buying a physical print of my art I'm happy to say that I have an online store.

I print all my pieces myself and ships them from Mölndal to ensure that I have full control over the quality of my work. The prints are made using a high end printer, archival grade inks and Hahnemühle fine-art papers.

Shipping is included and the prints are shipped using PostNord as soon as the print is ready. At this time I do not provide framing services for online orders due to the cost and difficulties of shipping framed items.

If you have any questions regarding prints or ordering please write to gallery@rahmn.net or DM on Instagram

Returns

Prints are examined carefully before shipping for any quality issues. In the unfortunate event that you do receive a damaged or defective print I ask that you contact me and provide pictures of the damage. Such damaged prints will be replaced free of charge or refunded if unable to provide a new print. The exception to this is if damaged arose due to mishandling by the customer.

Do note that inkjet prints are very delicate by nature and should be handled by the edges to avoid scratching or thumbprints on the print surface. To ensure longevity of artwork I highly recommend framing it behind glass or alternatively laminating it. If you have any questions regarding care or framing don't hesitate to contact me.

If you are unhappy with your print for any reasons you have the right to revoke the purchase within 14 days of receiving it. In such a case write to gallery@rahmn.net to arrange return and refund. In this case the return shipping cost is covered by the customer.
